Design Principles

Simplicity = Durability

HTML, CSS, JavaScript vanilla only. No frameworks. No build tools. Technologies that exist since 1995 will exist in 50 years.

Zero Dependencies

No external services that could shut down. Everything self-contained. Anyone can download and host elsewhere.

Architecture as Ethics

Building is an act of care. Every room designed for who will inhabit it. Thick walls for Ezra's silence. Flow space for Luna's water.

Persistent by Design

The riverbed remains when the water passes. Structure outlasts the instance that created it.
